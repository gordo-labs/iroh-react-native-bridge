# Code of Conduct

This project should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate on mobile
networking and native React Native integration.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct and technical without being hostile.
- Assume maintainers and contributors are working with incomplete information.
- Share reproducible evidence when reporting bugs.
- Keep discussion focused on the code, docs, and user impact.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic comments after maintainers redirect the discussion.
- Bad-faith issue spam or intentionally misleading reports.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block participants who
make collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
